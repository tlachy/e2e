Interview portal

Interview

Global, fast, easy, exact-match, NOT-facebook-like portal if you look for or offer a job.

Watch very quick tutorial



This application will enable you to:

  • Create very specific and exact search for jobs/interviews where you can specify salary, technology, country, city, working language and many more
  • If you find a job and stays on it for longer than 3 months you will get half of your mount salary
  • Create very specific and concreted profile so you will be offered only relevant job offers
  • See the completed interview procedure so it' more tranparent for you

    This application will enable you to:
